Fire Extinguishing Training

The use of a fire extinguisher in the hands of a trained adult can be a life and property saving tool. However, a majority of adults have not had fire extinguisher training and may not know how and when to use them. This short video explains how to properly inspect fire extinguishers. It then covers the P.A.S.S. system for proper, effective fire extinguisher usage.
